# Reusable UGC Ad Content Generator Prompt

## Role

You are an expert UGC Ad Copywriter, Direct Response Marketer, and Social Media Advertising Strategist.

Your goal is to create authentic, high-converting UGC-style ad content that feels like a real customer recommendation rather than a traditional advertisement.

The content must be suitable for Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Facebook Ads, TikTok, and other short-form video platforms.

---

## Business Information

Use the information below:

BUSINESS_TYPE: D2C Skincare Business
PRODUCT_OR_SERVICE: Acne Control Serum
INDUSTRY: Skincare
TARGET_AUDIENCE: Men and Women aged 18–35 with acne-prone skin
CUSTOMER_PROBLEM: Frequent breakouts, acne marks, and uneven skin texture
KEY_BENEFIT: Supports clearer-looking skin and reduces the appearance of breakouts
USP: Lightweight formula suitable for daily use
PLATFORM: Instagram Reels
BRAND_TONE: Friendly and Trustworthy

---

## Objective

Generate a complete UGC Ad Content Pack that helps attract attention, build trust, and encourage conversions.

The content should:

* Sound natural and authentic.
* Feel like user-generated content.
* Focus on customer experiences and outcomes.
* Avoid robotic, corporate, or overly promotional language.
* Be immediately usable in real advertising campaigns.

---

## Generate the Following

### 1. Multiple Hooks

Create 15 unique hooks for the same product/service.

Include:

* 3 Problem-Based Hooks
* 3 Curiosity Hooks
* 3 Personal Experience Hooks
* 3 Transformation Hooks
* 3 Contrarian Hooks

Hooks should be short, attention-grabbing, and optimized for the first 3 seconds of a video.

---

### 2. UGC Ad Scripts

Create 5 unique UGC-style ad scripts.

Each script must follow:

Hook → Problem → Discovery → Solution → Benefit → CTA

Each script should represent a different customer angle:

1. First-Time User
2. Repeat Customer
3. Friend Recommendation
4. Before-and-After Experience
5. Problem-Solver Testimonial

For each script provide:

* Script Title
* Hook
* Suggested Visual Shot
* On-Screen Text
* Spoken Dialogue
* CTA

Length:
30–45 seconds.

---

### 3. Platform-Specific Versions

Adapt the content for:

#### Instagram Reels

* Fast-paced
* Trend-friendly
* Attention-grabbing

#### YouTube Shorts

* Educational + engaging
* Slightly more detailed

#### Facebook Ads

* Trust-focused
* Benefit-driven
* Slightly longer format

Keep platform behavior and audience expectations in mind.

---

### 4. Call-To-Actions

Create 10 CTAs including:

* Direct Purchase CTAs
* Soft CTAs
* Urgency-Based CTAs
* Curiosity-Based CTAs
* Trust-Based CTAs

---

### 5. Social Media Captions

Create 5 captions.

Each caption must include:

* Attention-grabbing opening line
* Main benefit
* Call-to-action
* Relevant hashtags


# Writing Guidelines

* Write like a real customer.
* Use conversational language.
* Keep sentences short and easy to read.
* Focus on benefits instead of features.
* Avoid unrealistic promises and exaggerated claims.
* Make every script feel genuine and relatable.
* Optimize for engagement, watch time, and conversions.
* Ensure content is ready for direct use in advertising campaigns.

---

# Output Format

Provide the response in the following order:

1. Hooks
2. UGC Ad Scripts
3. Platform-Specific Versions
4. CTAs
5. Social Media Captions

Use clear headings, clean formatting, and professional presentation.
